How To Play
  1. Start the Game
    • Click on the "Play" button on the welcome screen to start the game.
  2. Objective
    • Your objective in this game is to safely bring the bunny to its home by matching pairs of carrot cards and earning points while avoiding traps and dog cards.
  3. Matching Carrots
    • Matching pairs of carrot cards earns you 10 points. Keep an eye out for these cards as they are your key to scoring.
  4. Avoiding Traps
    • Matching trap cards results in a loss of 5 points. Be cautious and try to remember where these traps are located.
  5. Beware of Dog Cards
    • If you select a dog card, you lose the game. Dogs are your opponents in this memory game, so avoid them at all costs.
  6. Completing Levels
    • To win the game, you must successfully complete all levels. Completing all levels means the bunny reaches its hole, signifying your progress.
  7. Checking Your Score
    • Your current score is displayed on the game screen. Strive to achieve a high score by matching carrot pairs and avoiding traps.
  8. Using the Restart Button
    • If you wish to start over, you can click the "Restart" button on the game screen.
  9. Exit the Game
    • To exit the game, simply click the "Exit" button on the welcome screen.
  10. Learn More with the Info Button
    • If you need additional instructions or information about the game, click the "Info" button on either the welcome screen or the game screen.
